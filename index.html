Here's all the stuff about the game.

A game of chess with a built in library of opening moves that allow you to play while identify those moves.

Moves Library

The app will provide a library with chess opening moves, a brief description, history and a youtube video.

Practice mode

The app will provide a pratice mode so you can learn the opening moves against a AI opponent.

Player vs Player

The app will provide a PvP mode so you can challenge your friends and invite them to play with you.
